<p>Ancient Greek Lexicon &amp; Syntax is an open source tool for students of ancient Greek. It contains a full, searchable copy of Liddell and Scott’s <i>Intermediate Greek-English Lexicon,</i> as well as the text of Jeffrey A. Rydberg-Cox’s <i>Overview of Greek Syntax.</i></p><p><i>Greek Reference</i> is a complete, searchable, offline Greek lexicon featuring flexible lexicon search with instant search suggestions, Bookmarks for your favorite lexicon entries and <i>Overview of Greek Syntax</i> sections, and more.</p>
